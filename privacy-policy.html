Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 13, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Password Generator protects your privacy.

1. Data Collection and Storage

We are committed to your privacy. Password Generator:

  • Generates passwords 100% locally on your device - No passwords are sent to any server
  • Stores password history locally using AsyncStorage on your device only
  • Does not collect, store, or transmit personal information
  • Does not use analytics, tracking, or advertising

2. Subscription and Payment

Password Generator offers an optional Pro subscription through Apple In-App Purchase:

  • Monthly Plan: $0.99/month
  • Yearly Plan: $4.99/year

Your subscription is managed by Apple. We use RevenueCat as a service layer with anonymous user IDs only.

3. Third-Party Services

RevenueCat: Manages App Store subscriptions with strict privacy standards. Only anonymous subscription status is tracked.

Apple App Store: Payment processing handled entirely by Apple.

4. Data Security

  • All password generation happens offline on your device
  • Password history is protected by iOS built-in security
  • We cannot access your passwords - only you can
  • Uninstalling the app deletes all data from your device

5. What We Do Not Do

  • We do not collect cookies, trackers, or identifiers
  • We do not sell, share, or trade your data
  • We do not use third-party analytics
  • We do not show advertisements
  • We do not create user profiles or accounts
Bottom Line: Your passwords stay on your device. We cannot access them, and they are never sent anywhere.

6. Children's Privacy

Password Generator is appropriate for all ages. We do not collect any personal data from anyone.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.
